# 📊 Superstore Sales & Profitability Dashboard (Excel)

An interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard analyzing **4 years of retail sales data (2014–2017)** to uncover profitability trends, identify loss-making products, and provide actionable business recommendations.

The project combines **Power Query**, **PivotTables**, **PivotCharts**, **Slicers**, and dashboard design principles to transform raw transactional data into an executive-level business dashboard.

> **Dataset:** ~10,000 order records from the popular Superstore dataset.

# 🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze historical retail sales data and answer key business questions related to:

* Revenue growth
* Profitability
* Product performance
* Regional performance
* Customer segmentation
* Discount strategy

The dashboard is designed for business users who need quick, interactive insights to support decision-making.

---

# 📌 Executive Summary

Although the company generated over **$2.29M in sales** and **$286K in profit**, profitability is not evenly distributed.

The analysis reveals that **Furniture**, particularly **Tables** and **Bookcases**, consistently generates losses due to excessive discounting. Meanwhile, **Technology products** such as Copiers and Phones contribute significantly to overall profits.

This demonstrates that improving pricing strategy in a few underperforming product groups could substantially increase company profitability.

# 🔍 Key Insights

### 📌 Overall Business Performance

* Generated $2.29M in total sales.
* Earned $286K profit.
* Overall profit margin of 12.5%.

---

### 📌 Product Performance

Top Profit Contributors

* Copiers
* Phones
* Accessories

Loss-Making Products

* Tables
* Bookcases
* Supplies

Despite generating strong sales, **Tables lose approximately $17.7K**, making them the biggest profitability concern.

---

### 📌 Regional Performance

* West Region delivers the highest overall profit.
* East Region also performs strongly.
* Central and South regions underperform in comparison.

---

### 📌 Customer Segments

The **Consumer** segment contributes the largest share of both sales and profits, making it the company's most valuable customer group.

---

### 📌 Discount Analysis

The dashboard reveals a strong negative relationship between **Discount** and **Profit Margin**.

Higher discounts consistently reduce profitability, especially within Furniture products.

---

# 💼 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

* Reduce excessive discounting on Tables and Bookcases.
* Focus marketing efforts on high-margin Technology products.
* Review pricing strategy in underperforming regions.
* Monitor discount thresholds to protect profit margins.
* Expand inventory for consistently profitable products.
